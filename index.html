Abby Nelson, whimsical artist/designer focused on establishing empathy and storyboard building to enhance the UX/UI experience. Design should be inclusive, accessible, and thoughtful. Using careful research from users, creating personas, journeys, storyboards and scenarios we can tune in to the true depth a users need through their own experiences...Thereby creating an experience for the user they may never even have considered themselves. Using the most state of the art collaborative programs such as figma, miro, inVision, Visual Studio and AdobeXD... Several community-influcing and changin works have been created.
